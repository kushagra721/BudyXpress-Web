About Us

BuddyXpress is India’s first app-based platform crafted to help people find real companionship for everyday moments. From street food crawls to monument hopping, shopping sessions to spontaneous sports activities, we connect you with trusted companions for authentic, judgment-free experiences.

Inspired by Japan’s culture, our service bridges loneliness and social gaps in a safe, respectful way. Whether you're new to a city, just need someone to explore with, or want to break your routine—BuddyXpress is here for you.

We are not a dating or escort app. Every companion undergoes a rigorous verification process to ensure your comfort and safety.

Lets keep depression away. Join us as we redefine how India connects—through shared adventures, good vibes, and meaningful interactions.
